App Support

Common questions and troubleshooting

Bluetooth button not connecting?

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone, and pair the button in System Settings → Bluetooth. The app will detect the button automatically after the first press.

Audio not playing?

Check your phone's volume and ensure the app has audio permissions enabled in Settings → LobeDrive.

How do I change language?

Open the app → Settings (gear icon) → tap the language toggle to switch between Swedish and English.

Privacy concerns?

LobeDrive collects zero data. Everything works offline. See our Privacy Policy for details.
