Troubleshooting

Button won't enter pairing mode

  • Check if the button has battery power (replace battery if needed)
  • Hold the button longer (some buttons require 5-10 seconds)
  • Consult your button's user manual for specific pairing instructions

Button pairs but doesn't trigger the app

  • Make sure LobeDrive is running (open the app)
  • Check that Bluetooth permissions are enabled for LobeDrive in Settings â†’ LobeDrive â†’ Bluetooth
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting the button in your phone's Bluetooth settings
  • Restart the app and try again

No audio playing through car speakers

  • Verify your phone is connected to your car's Bluetooth audio system
  • Check your car's audio source is set to "Bluetooth" (not FM, USB, etc.)
  • Increase phone volume and car volume
  • Test by playing music from your phoneâ€”if that works, LobeDrive audio will too

Connection drops frequently

  • Make sure the button is within range (typically 10 meters / 30 feet)
  • Replace the button's battery if it's low
  • Forget the device in Bluetooth settings and re-pair from scratch
  • Close other Bluetooth-heavy apps that might interfere

App shows "Not connected" even after pairing

  • Press the button once while the app is openâ€”the app detects the button on first press
  • Wait up to 30 seconds after pressing for the status to update
  • Check that the button is still paired in your phone's Bluetooth settings
