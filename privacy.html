Privacy Policy (LobeDrive)

Last updated: December 2025

LobeDrive ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store personal data when you visit our website, use our mobile app, join our waitlist, or contact us.

We operate in Sweden, and all processing of personal data follows the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Swedish law.

1. Data We Collect

1A. Website Data

We only collect the minimum personal data necessary for operating the website and delivering our waitlist service:

  • Name (optional on some forms)
  • Email address (for the waitlist and communication)
  • Message content if you contact us via email or form
  • Basic website analytics (non-identifiable, aggregated usage data)

1B. Mobile App Data

The LobeDrive mobile app is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • No personal data is collected or transmitted from the app
  • All audio files are stored locally on your device
  • Your language preference, settings, and usage history remain on your device only
  • The app functions fully offline â€“ no internet connection required
  • No analytics, tracking, or data sharing occurs through the app

The only data stored are:

  • Language preference (Swedish/English) â€“ locally on your device
  • Onboarding completion status â€“ locally on your device
  • Bluetooth button connection timestamp (if used) â€“ locally on your device

We cannot access this data. It never leaves your device.

1C. Bluetooth Button Usage

If you choose to use a Bluetooth button with LobeDrive:

  • The connection is local between your phone and the button
  • We do not collect or store information about your button usage
  • Button press timestamps are stored locally on your device only
  • No data about your driving or button usage is sent to our servers

We do not collect sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the website
  • To manage and communicate with our waitlist subscribers
  • To reply to questions or partnership inquiries
  • To keep the service secure and prevent misuse
  • To comply with legal obligations under Swedish/EU law

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process personal data based on:

  • Consent (when you join the waitlist or contact us voluntarily)
  • Legitimate interest (for basic site analytics and security)
  • Legal obligation (when required under Swedish or EU law)

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your information is stored securely within the EU.
We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected for:

  • Waitlist information: stored until you unsubscribe or until the product launches
  • Communications: stored as long as needed to manage your inquiry
  • Website analytics data: stored only in aggregated, non-personal form
  • Mobile app data: stored locally on your device only (we have no access)

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • File a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY)

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

6. Cookies

Our website may use simple cookies for:

  • Basic functionality
  • Analytics in aggregated form

The mobile app does not use cookies.

You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

7A. Website Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools for:

  • Website hosting
  • Email communication
  • Analytics

These providers follow GDPR and process data only on our behalf.

We do not use advertising cookies or data-sharing trackers.

7B. Mobile App Third-Party Services

The mobile app does NOT use any third-party services or SDKs.

All functionality is built-in and operates entirely offline.

7C. iOS Platform and App Store

The LobeDrive app:

  • Requests Bluetooth permission only (if you choose to use a Bluetooth button)
  • Does not share data with Apple beyond standard App Store requirements
  • May be backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud Backup enabled (your choice via iOS settings)
  • Complies with Apple's App Store Privacy Requirements

8. Children's Privacy

LobeDrive is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
