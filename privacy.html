Privacy Policy (LobeDrive)

Last updated: November 2025

LobeDrive ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store personal data when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or contact us.

We operate in Sweden, and all processing of personal data follows the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Swedish law.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect the minimum personal data necessary for operating the website and delivering our waitlist service:

  • Name (optional on some forms)
  • Email address (for the waitlist and communication)
  • Message content if you contact us via email or form
  • Basic website analytics (non-identifiable, aggregated usage data)

We do not collect sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the website
  • To manage and communicate with our waitlist subscribers
  • To reply to questions or partnership inquiries
  • To keep the service secure and prevent misuse
  • To comply with legal obligations under Swedish/EU law

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process personal data based on:

  • Consent (when you join the waitlist or contact us voluntarily)
  • Legitimate interest (for basic site analytics and security)
  • Legal obligation (when required under Swedish or EU law)

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your information is stored securely within the EU.
We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected for:

  • Waitlist information: stored until you unsubscribe or until the product launches
  • Communications: stored as long as needed to manage your inquiry
  • Analytics data: stored only in aggregated, non-personal form

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • File a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY)

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

6. Cookies

Our website may use simple cookies for:

  • Basic functionality
  • Analytics in aggregated form

You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools for:

  • Website hosting
  • Email communication
  • Analytics

These providers follow GDPR and process data only on our behalf.

We do not use advertising cookies or data-sharing trackers.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed. The updated version will always be available on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
