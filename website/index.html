Anger management for drivers

You're stuck in traffic. Someone cuts you off. Your heart races. Your jaw clenches. The anger comes fast—and suddenly, you're not the calm driver you want to be.

Road rage isn't just frustrating. It's dangerous. It affects your judgment, your reactions, and your safety. And in that moment, when you need help most, there's nothing to turn to.

Until now.

Your brain on road rage.

When anger hits, your amygdala (your brain's alarm system) takes over. It's fast, reactive, and emotional.

Your frontal lobe—the part responsible for rational thinking and self-control—gets pushed aside.

LobeDrive reactivates your frontal lobe. A brief pause. A moment to breathe. A chance to think before you react.

That's the difference between escalation and control.

"LobeDrive" = Lobe + Drive. Use your frontal lobe. Drive smarter.
